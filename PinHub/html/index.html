What is PinHub?

Imagine Pinterest and GitHub combined. My goal is to make a social media network for Coders, Tech DIYers, Nurds, ect. I personaly hate having a Coder's block, Through this I plan to get ideas or even help. Pinhub is for open source developer, So if you need help just click the help buttion on your post and wait for someone to find it on their feed.
